Customer Relationship Management

By the name itself, Customer Relationship Management is the relationship between a customer and a company. CRM is the amalgamation of techniques, strategies, and technology used by a company to maintain its relationship with its customer.

A CRM system will compile information from the company’s database like website, email, telephone, social media followers, chats etc., to better understand their customer’s product usage life-cycle and pitch them the product accordingly. In this way, the customer feels more important as they know that the company is listening to them and the company gets loyal customers.

So, to frame it better, CRM is a win-win situation for both parties.
