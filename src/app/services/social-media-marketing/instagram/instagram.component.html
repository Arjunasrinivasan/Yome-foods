Instagram

Since it first came in 2010, seems like everyone loves using Instagram. Instagram’s reach is enormous, just like Facebook, Instagram has over 1 billion active users and there are almost 500 million stories every day. It looks like Instagram was made for food lovers and vloggers, as Instagram has its own community when it comes to food.

This is the best place to upload short videos of your dish or sneak peak of your restaurant, because Instagrammers love these types of videos. Recipe videos, sneak peak, new offer, new restaurant, festive discount, engagement posts, FAQs, etc., these are the trending things that tops Instagram.

This clearly has opened up new opportunities for marketing where you can reach a larger audience. Instagram has an ad manager similar to Facebook, but you can also post your ads through Facebook’s ad manager. As per research, 80% of Instagrammers follow at least one food profile, so here if your restaurant posts new photos and stories about your daily business, your followers will be notified immediately, and even if your audience may not visit, at least you have created awareness about your restaurant. Apart from celebrities, there are Instagram Influencers who can do paid promotions with your brand and collaborate with your brand and give you a new set of audience. Social trends and campaigns happen every now and then, so if your brand can follow these trends, your brand will be topping the charts.
