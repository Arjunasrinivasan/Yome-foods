Facebook

If you are starting with social media marketing then you probably know that you need to be on Facebook. It is the biggest social media marketing platform. With this added benefit of large audience, you can use Facebook for promoting anything that is new in your restaurant, you can create events and invite people, and you can create polls and ask your loyal patrons for valuable feedback. This will make your customers feel like you are listening to them and they will remain loyal to your business.

On Facebook you can create your own company profile and promote your business through Facebook ad manager, you can specifically target your audience and know the exact engagement, likes and dislikes of your targeted audience. Here you have more chance of people sharing your post and creating self-promotion.

Apart from promoting your page and posting ads, you can also create Facebook groups to get a better understanding of the market. Social media marketing on Facebook can be done through images, ads, videos, group discussions etc. Facebook is the king of social media marketing with end number of possibilities because you can do marketing in your demographics and also worldwide, which can create new opportunities without spending extra bucks.
