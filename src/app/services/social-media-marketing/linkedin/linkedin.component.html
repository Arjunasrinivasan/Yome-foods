LinkedIn

LinkedIn is one of the largest professional networking platforms with over 260 million active users, and where there is an audience, there is always a scope of marketing. Marketing on LinkedIn is not like Facebook and Instagram, there is a huge difference between these social media platforms. LinkedIn is more professional than its counterpart Facebook, so restaurateur who wish to promote their business on LinkedIn needs to show themselves as professionals. There are many CEOs and VPs of different companies, who if likes your business idea, they can get in touch with you for collaboration. Marketing your restaurant on LinkedIn can really give you high class clientele and a new professional market. You can easily personalise your profile and connect with different groups to create engagement and create brand awareness.

LinkedIn is best known for lead generation and finding B2B—Business to Business—collaborations; as per research, 80% of B2B social leads are generated through LinkedIn. Yes, that surpasses Facebook, Twitter, and Instagram individually. For marketing your restaurant on LinkedIn you need to keep it very professional and accurate. Create your company profile and update it regularly, join groups, search for individuals etc., do everything in your capacity to keep it engaging.
