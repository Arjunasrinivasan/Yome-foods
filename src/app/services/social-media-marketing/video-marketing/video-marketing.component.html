Video Marketing on Social Media

Research shows by 2022, 82% of the Internet content will be in video format; around 1 billion hours of YouTube video is watched each day, which clearly indicates that marketing is no longer limited to TV ads and pamphlets. Video marketing on Instagram happens on IGTV, where restaurants can upload clips of food preparation and viewers can subscribe to their channel. In this way, restaurants can ensure that the viewer will be aware of their existence and will recognise the brand anywhere.

YouTube on the other-hand is a video-sharing platform where you can advertise your brand on any video that the viewer will watch. Video marketing on YouTube is a win-win situation for any brand. YouTube is best known for recipe videos and people turn to YouTube for learning new dishes.

WhatApp is direct marketing, as you can connect to your customer directly on their number. Marketing on WhatApp is simple and direct; here you can give that special treatment to your customer by giving them exclusive coupons and vouchers.
