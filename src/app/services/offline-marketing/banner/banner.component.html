Have you ever come across any food ads on the Internet? Sure you have; those boxes which appear on the web page when we surf the Internet are called Banners. These ads are extensively used on web pages that get a high volume of visitors. Whether you are on a restaurant website or some shopping website, these food ads can really make you click on them. These banner ads have text and images on them that literally satiate your taste buds by just looking at them; while some advertisers use flashy creative to attract the visitorâ€™s attention. Banner ads may vary; they can be static or animated depending on the restaurant and their cuisine.

But how are they paid? There are three basic types of methods: CPC- Cost per click means how many people clicked on your ad CPI- Cost per impression means how many people saw your ad CPA- Cost per action means how many people actually purchased the product that you are selling through this banner ad.

You can imagine it as a physical banner that we see on the streets or in front of a shop, it is exactly the same, but this banner is virtual and on the Internet. As soon as you click on these banner ads you will be directed to the homepage of the company, here you will learn more information about the ad.
