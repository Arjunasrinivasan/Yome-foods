Hoardings

Hoardings are outdoor advertisement and it is also known as billboard advertising in the US. They are giant posters that are mounted on the streets and building for everyone to see.

As a restaurant owner you can rely on hoardings if your restaurant is near the highway or on the crossroads, because hoardings are considered to be the super ad, as it has the highest audience reach. You can install hoarding a few kilometres before the actual location and literally inform everyone that your restaurant is nearby. Hungry travellers will and people who need refreshments will definitely take a halt at your restaurant.

If you have discounts to offer or a new cuisine, that can be the highlight of your ad, because people who travel would definitely want to try something new.

Hoarding allows reaching a wider audience of all gender and age. In other marketing styles like TV ads, magazines, Google ads etc., you reach out to the customer for advertisement; but in this marketing style, the audience comes to you.

Hoardings are the best way to attract more and more audience because of its visibility.
