Analytics & Insights

Almost every company has a department that solely focuses on Analytics and Insights. There are dedicated teams and experts whose only job is to analyse and provide insights, but why is it so important?

To put things into perspective, every company has to collect their data, whether it is sales data or production data etc., the information that has been extracted from this data is called analytics. Insight, on the other hand, is what you gain after understanding analytics.

Every business needs analytics and insights because with this information you can improvise your current data. E.g. with the analytics and insights, a company can cut down on unnecessary expenses, make faster and better decisions or enhance their new product. These insights can help you to improve your sales, strategy and help you better market your product.

Analytics and Insights are very essential to grow as a business and to understand the market; it is not related to a specific field or industry, this helps in every industry.
