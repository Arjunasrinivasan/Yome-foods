Internet of Things

We are surrounded by things that can function for our convenience, but we still constantly try to enhance it. Till a certain extent, we have successfully taught these things to communicate within themselves, and this has changed the way we use them and live our daily lives.

The Internet of things is changing our lives, and we can barely notice it; by the definition, the Internet of Things is the interconnection of devices via the Internet enabling them to send and receive data. So, the Internet of Things is a platform where devices can dump their data and then with the help of the Internet of Things, they can communicate themselves.

E.g. your phone is always connected to the GPS, so it knows where it is, and this information is communicated from the phone to your smart lights at home. As soon as reach home, the lights are turned on as per your convenience.

In this example, your phone was able to communicate to the smart lights at home with the help of the Internet of Things and let it know that you are arriving.
