Blockchain

By the name itself, a blockchain is quite literally a chain of blocks, but here we are talking about digital information (block) and public database (chain). In simple words, blockchain is basically a way of documenting information on the internet; a ledger, which is highly secure and very difficult to tamper with, because every block in that chain is connected to the previous block and if you want to hack one block you will have to change the entire chain.

As it is a chain, it can be traced anywhere you want. So, once the information is noted in the blockchain, it will remain there forever. Blockchain technology is used in the banking industry and soon it may be used in other industries as well. Like it can be used for storing medical history or tax purpose as well.

Like the Internet changed our lives within the past 20 years, blockchain will in the next 20 years.
