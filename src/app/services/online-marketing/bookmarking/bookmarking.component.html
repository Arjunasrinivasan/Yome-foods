Bookmarking

If you are an avid reader, you may be familiar with the term ‘Bookmarking’. Just like we bookmark our favourite page in a physical book, bookmarking in the digital world means exactly the same. It is also called as Social Bookmarking.

We normally send food tips like nutrition facts and which dish to eat for better diet etc., to our friends and family; but what we don’t know here is that we are inadvertently taking part in Social Bookmarking. In simple words, the process of sharing and tagging webpages is called Social Bookmarking.

There are a few famous websites that you might have heard of but barely knew that they are a social bookmarking website, like Pinterest, Bitly, Evernote, Delicious, etc. There are end numbers of webpages that are bookmarked in food category because we tend to read, watch and share food video.

These websites keep a track of bookmarked pages so that it is simple for its users to find valuable information. They categorise bookmarked websites in sections like technology, science, travel, food, politics, etc., which also helps in terms of SEO in the long run.
