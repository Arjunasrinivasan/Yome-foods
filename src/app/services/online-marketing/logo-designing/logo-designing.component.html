Logo Designing

The name of a brand is its identity, but the Logo is the face of the brand. A logo should signify the value of its company which will distinguish it from its competitors and let it stand out in the market. Designing a restaurant logo needs brainstorming with a succinct strategy that will help the customers to identify the restaurant even without its name, and this, in turn, will create a strong brand identity. Once you have a strong brand identity, you can use this goodwill for online marketing; like McDonald's, Dominos, Starbucks, etc., these brands do not need to mention their name, and their logo is enough for the customer to recognise them.

Just displaying the logo will signify simplicity and builds a trust factor within its targeted audience.
