Blogging

It is safe to say that blogging was the first step towards the cosmic web of social media and digital marketing. Before social media, blogging was the only way to pen your thoughts and share it with others, which is now on your fingertips. There are umpteen food bloggers who blog about recipes, nutrition facts and best dish in different restaurants. But how is blogging going to help you from a business standpoint of view?

Blogging about a dish for a restaurant can really boast your webpage because people are interested in knowing where the best food in their location is, and helps you to attract more traffic and divert more audience to your website. People who read food blogs or who needs some information about some dish or restaurant will land on your blog page and this will help you to churn these readers into potential customers.

Blogging also helps in SEO; if your website has an ample amount of content—blogs—with proper SEO, it is easy to stumble upon your webpage rather than any other. E.g., if someone is looking for some local delicacy, and you have a blog with the same keywords, it is highly possible that the reader will land your website because your SEO is strong and correct. In this way, you will gain traffic and the reader will learn the information that he/she is looking for
