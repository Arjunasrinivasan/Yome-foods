Visiting Cards

A visiting card or a calling card is a business or personal card that bears the contact information of a specific person with the restaurant's details. They are shared during a customer's visit as a memory aid.

Visiting cards are an essential part of marketing, looking at its size and cost, it is the best way to promote your restaurant. When the food you prepare is delicious, people tend to remember your restaurant by the taste and there are high chances that they can give you bulk orders or take you in for some catering purpose. This will be possible only if they remember your restaurant's name; for that purpose, we have a visiting card. As visiting cards are short and sweet, you cannot expect it to tell the entire story, but at least that person can remember that they visited your restaurant and these cards can leave a memorable impact too.

There are multiple types of cards that are used in the market today, and obviously, there is always an option to create a card that suits your restaurant's style but remembers the more unique it is, greater the impact will be.
