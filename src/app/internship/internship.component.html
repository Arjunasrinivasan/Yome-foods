About the Company

PharmEasy is a medicine delivery mobile app which delivers medicine at your doorstep within 24 hours without any delivery charges. Currently delivering in 12 major cities with cash on delivery option available. For more details: https://pharmeasy.in

Roles & Responsibilities

  1. You will be responsible for acquiring new customers for the company PharmEasy
  2. You need to approach potential customers in & around your location, pitch them the advantages of ordering medicines from PharmEasy App
  3. Convince the customer to download the PharmEasy App and make sure they place their first order by using a special 20% discount promo code (promo code will be provided to you post your selection)
  4. Enter necessary details (like name, contact no. etc.) of the customer in the Awign App
  5. You will be trained online by the respective manager on all the aspects of this project including sales pitch, before you start your internship

You will be paid on the basis of number of conversions you do

conversions- refers to successful delivery of the order placed by a customer using your promo code.

What you get?

  5. Dedicated manager for one-on-one mentorship throughout the course of your internship
